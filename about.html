About LA Fitness

Founded in Southern California in 1984, LA Fitness has grown into one of the most popular fitness clubs in North America, with over 700 clubs across the United States and Canada. Our goal is to help people of all ages and fitness levels achieve their health goals in a supportive and welcoming environment.

Our History

LA Fitness was born out of a need for a fitness center that provides a full-service gym experience at an affordable price. Starting in a small gym space, LA Fitness quickly expanded due to its commitment to quality fitness equipment, diverse group classes, and excellent customer service. By the 1990s, the brand began acquiring and renovating existing clubs, establishing itself as a trusted name in the fitness industry.

Today, LA Fitness continues to innovate by adding new equipment, modern facilities, and training programs to meet the evolving needs of our members. From weightlifting and cardio equipment to personal training and swimming pools, our gyms provide a complete workout experience.
